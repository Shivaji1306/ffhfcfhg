Our Story

SereniTea started as a weekend hobby among friends who loved the ritual of tea. Today we source small-batch teas from trusted farms and package them with care.

Sourcing with integrity

We partner with small growers who prioritize sustainable practices. Each harvest is tasted and curated before joining our selection.

Craft and care

From leaf to cup, we focus on freshness. Our teas are stored in airtight packaging, labeled with harvest dates, and shipped quickly.
